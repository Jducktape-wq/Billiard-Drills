Privacy Policy — Rack & Run

Effective date: July 3, 2026

Summary

Rack & Run does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data. Period.

What data the app uses

Your practice history (drill attempts, makes and misses, best sets) is stored locally on your device using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device, is never sent to any server, and is not visible to the developer or anyone else.

What we don't do

The app contains no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, no cookies, and no third-party data sharing. It does not access your location, contacts, camera, microphone, or any other device features.

Data deletion

Uninstalling the app, or clearing the app's site data in your device settings, permanently deletes all locally stored practice history.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the developer contact listed on the app's Google Play page.
